I want to tell you about my dream. From the age of 9 I dreamed of becoming a game developer, I also dreamed of developing a banking system, but as a child I dreamed of a cat, and at the age of 15 I got a cat, when I adopted him, he was 2 months old and he was afraid of everything.

But at 2 years old my cat became seriously ill and my life turned upside down, every day doctors fought for his little life.

every month there were relapses of his illness and after 3 long years my cat is healthy, during these 3 years he went through 4 serious operations, each operation was stressful for him and me.

My cat is already 5 years old and now he enjoys life and believes that miracles do happen. Therefore, believe in yourself, dream. A miracle will always come to you even if there is no way out.
